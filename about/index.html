I am Yu-Ching (林于靖), a UX designer.

The reason that I love UX design is that it requires many considerations. In this role, I need to review every aspect of each project thoroughly, which helps cultivate open-mindedness and tolerance of failures. With each completed project, my thinking process improves.

I received my degree in Psychology from National Cheng Kung University. Throughout the course of my studies, I learned how to conduct psychological experiments, analyze data, and draw conclusions. This robust foundation has many applications and parallels in understanding and conducting user research.

I was born and raised in Taiwan with a great zeal for exploring the world and learning. As a self-motivated worker, I continue to pursue learning opportunities for cutting-edge design theory and front-end techniques. I enjoy working with a combination of elements when solving problems or creating user-centered designs.
