Hello, I'm Yu-Ching Lin.

Currently a UX designer at Synology based in Taipei.

As a lover of psychology, I spent my college years studying the subject and delving deep into understanding human behavior. I was fascinated by the ways in which psychological principles could be applied to real-world challenges — and this passion led me to pursue a career in UX.

Throughout my professional journey, I have tackled complex projects involving both B2B technical and B2C consumer-facing products. The most rewarding aspect of my work is identifying users' needs and translating them into intuitive interfaces that influence human behavior.

I'm passionate about unleashing my creativity. I love reading and writing because it helps me connect my ideas and come up with new ones. In my personal life, I'm always experimenting with new ways to improve myself — like keeping my surroundings clean, saving money, and staying active.
